Is your Chiller Dying? Maybe not!

DO YOU HAVE...

  • An HVAC system installed more than 10 years back?
  • Frequent complaint of equipment breakdown?
  • Increasing expenses for HVAC repairs and spare parts?

Now You have an option

You can have your old equipment modernized and refurbished, at a fraction of the cost and minimal disruption. This is similar to a comprehensive overhaul of the entire machine with repair / replacement of major components and controls upgrades.

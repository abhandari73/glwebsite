AIRNEW Replacement / New Installation of HVAC Equipment

In certain cases, the cost-benefit analysis of modernization vs. replacement may favor replacing the equipment; for example:

  • Poor equipment maintenance history
  • Corrosive site conditions
  • Intensive end-use of equipment
  • Changes in site heat load conditions or redundancy requirements

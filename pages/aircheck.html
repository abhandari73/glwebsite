AIRCHECK

What We Do

Our service engineer audits your HVAC system / equipment with a comprehensive questionnaire to give you a detailed “health” report

  • Data readings for equipment technical parameters, ambient conditions and energy consumption
  • Tests for oil, water, vibration, thermography, etc.
  • Visual / photographic inspection of equipment condition
  • Information on site end-use, occupancy patterns, & site conditions
  • Log of previous utility bills, and equipment alarm / service history

The Results

Our report, along with photographs, data readings, test results, and checklists, highlights:

  • Deviations in equipment / system performance vs. design parameters and site operational duty
  • Root-cause analysis and impact (potential breakdowns, critical component failures, energy losses, indoor air quality, equipment life)
  • Corrective measures (repairs and maintenance) for optimal equipment / system performance
